Get involved

Our community runs on people who step up, to host a session, to offer a space, to sponsor, and simply to show up. Here's how you can help.

Host a session

We’re always looking for members to lead a session! No experience necessary. Whether it’s your first time or your fiftieth, we welcome you, and we’ll help you prepare, rehearse, or even co-host.

Sessions run 20–30 minutes; show & tell spots are just five. Bring anything our community would love.

Suggest a session

Offer a venue

Have a room for 20–40 people, near transit, with step-free access? You can host our next gathering. Community halls, cafés and workplaces make wonderful hosts. See the venue checklist for what we look for.

We’ll bring the community and the program; you bring the room. We always thank our hosts publicly by name.

Get in touch

Sponsor & support

Your Community is free to attend and runs on in-kind support. Sponsors can provide space, food or supplies, and are thanked by name with a link at the event and in our recap.
