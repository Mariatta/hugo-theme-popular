Fifteen of us spent a rainy Saturday exactly where we wanted to be: in front of very large tanks.

Highlights included the kelp forest exhibit, a spirited discussion about sump design, and one member taking ninety-two photos of the same anemone.

We left inspired, humbled, and with a shared list of wildly unrealistic dream-tank plans.
