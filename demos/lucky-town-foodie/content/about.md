Lucky Town Foodie Club started with six neighbours and one very ambitious lasagna. We're now a monthly gathering for cooks, bakers and enthusiastic eaters of every level.

No culinary skills necessary. Chopping onions badly next to someone chopping them well is how everyone here learned.
