We put on episode one of Reply 1988 "just to see the opening". Three hours later nobody had moved, and someone had adopted the entire Ssangmun-dong neighbourhood as their own family.

The first-crush debate got serious fast: hands were raised, sides were chosen, and the Junghwan vs. Taek question has been tabled for its own dedicated meetup because we ran out of evening before we ran out of opinions.

If you missed it: we're continuing with two episodes a week, the snack roster is in the club chat, and yes, 1988 you would have had a crush too.
