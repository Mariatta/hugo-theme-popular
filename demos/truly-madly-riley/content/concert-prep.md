## The week before

{{% checklist key="cp-week" %}}
Confirm the ticket is in the app, then screenshot it anyway.
Relisten to the last two albums, openers lean on them.
Check the venue's bag policy and plan the smallest possible bag.
Arrange the day off after. You will not be functional.
{{% /checklist %}}

## The day of

{{% checklist key="cp-dayof" %}}
Charge the phone to 100% and pack a power bank.
Hydrate like an athlete, queue like a professional.
Make one queue friend, concerts are a community sport.
Set the phone to airplane mode during the acoustic numbers, be present.
{{% /checklist %}}

{{% callout tone="warn" title="The one forgotten ticket incident" %}}
It was 2019. The box office was kind. The lesson is item one, and it is not optional.
{{% /callout %}}

## After

{{% checklist key="cp-after" %}}
Transcribe the setlist before sleep, memory is a liar.
Back up the photos and pick three for the concert diary.
Write the diary post within a week, feelings have a half-life.
Start monitoring tour rumours for the next one.
{{% /checklist %}}
